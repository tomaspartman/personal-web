The Lord of the Rings

J.R.R. Tolkien's masterpiece continues to captivate readers decades after its publication. This epic fantasy trilogy follows the journey of Frodo Baggins and the Fellowship as they embark on a quest to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth from the Dark Lord Sauron.

Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

Yuval Noah Harari's groundbreaking work traces the evolution of Homo sapiens from insignificant primates to the dominant species on Earth. Through engaging storytelling and thought-provoking analysis, Harari explores how cognitive, agricultural, and scientific revolutions shaped human civilization.

Atomic Habits

James Clear's practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones has transformed millions of lives. Based on scientific research and real-world examples, Atomic Habits reveals how tiny changes can lead to remarkable results.
